Installation steps

1
Open Power BI Desktop and go to your report.
2
In the Visualizations panel, click the three dots (···) → "Import a visual from a file".
3
Select the downloaded MerlinPowerBI_v3.pbiviz file and confirm.
4
Add the Merlin visual to your report, load your datasets and paste your license key in the activation bar.
