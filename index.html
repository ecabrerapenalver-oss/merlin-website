FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Does it work with any Power BI dataset?
Yes. Merlin works with any table you load into Power BI. Simply drag your fields into the Dataset slots and start asking questions.
Is my data secure?
Your data is sent securely over HTTPS to process your query and is never stored on our servers. Each query is processed in real-time and discarded immediately.
What happens when I reach my query limit?
Merlin will notify you inside the visual. Your limit resets every 30 days from your subscription date. You can upgrade anytime for more queries.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your Stripe customer portal at any time and you won't be charged again.
How do I install it in Power BI?
Download the .pbiviz file, go to Power BI Desktop ‚Üí Insert ‚Üí More visuals ‚Üí Import from file. Takes under a minute.
What languages does Merlin understand?
Merlin understands any language ‚Äî English, Spanish, French, German, and more. Ask your questions in whatever language feels natural.
