HOW IT WORKS

Merlin integrates directly into Power BI — no external apps, no complex setup.

01

Import the visual

Download the Merlin .pbiviz file and import it into Power BI Desktop from the Visualizations panel.

02

Connect your data

Add up to 5 data fields — dependent or independent variables — directly from your existing datasets.

03

Activate your license

Paste your license key in the activation bar and click Activate. Done in seconds.

04

Ask anything

Type your question in natural language. Merlin analyzes your data, interprets results and generates charts automatically.

GETTING YOUR LICENSE

From purchase to activation in under 2 minutes.

1

Choose your plan

Go to merlinpowerbi.com and select the plan that fits your needs — from the free Demo to Business.

2

Complete payment

Enter your details and pay securely via Stripe. Your license key will arrive by email within seconds.

3

Download the visual

Go to merlinpowerbi.com/download and download the MerlinPowerBI.pbiviz file.

4

Import into Power BI

Open Power BI Desktop → Visualizations panel → click ··· → Import a visual from a file → select the .pbiviz file.

5

Activate your license

Add Merlin to your report, paste your license key in the activation bar and click Activate. You're ready to go.

6

Manage your subscription

To cancel, change plan or update payment details, visit the customer portal.
